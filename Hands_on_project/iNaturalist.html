iNaturalist is, in their own words, “An online social network of people sharing biodiversity information to help each other learn about nature”. The goal of iNaturalist is to collect data and sightings of organisms in order to both help people connect to their local ecosystems as well as provide data for scientists and researchers to assist with conservation efforts. By adding observations or identifying species within other’s observations, you can directly contribute to the understanding of our local ecosystem!
